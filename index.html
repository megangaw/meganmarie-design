About Me

Hello, My name is Megan Gaw and I am a Graphic designer, I love all things design including, Typography, Logo/Branding, Web Design and illustration. Now I know every design page you go to is going to say how amazing they are at all these things and yes, I love all of it but my true passion, that thing I do for fun is hand lettering. Lettering is a relaxing activity I do on almost a daily basis. When you get that designer block that’s when I get my sketch book out and draw letters, either on their own or little quotes from the web.

A little on my background, I am from a small town just outside Ottawa called Almonte. Born and raised in the middle of the bush, finding any tree I could to climb up with my sketch book and draw for hours. I found a love for design at a young age, and my teachers helped me as much as they could along the way, including painting logos on the back wall when I should be in construction tech with the boys. Along the way I have learned so much and met so many amazing people, I cannot wait to show you what I’ve been up to!
